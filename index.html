Why ‘nwhacks’?

As the largest hackathon in the Pacific NorthWest Region, nwHacks is the place to be for the top hackers from Seattle, Vancouver, Victoria and Portland.

What is a ‘Hackathon’?

A Hackathon is an event where teams of 2-5 people create a software/hardware app in a limited amonut of time. Prizes are awarded to the most thoughtout, creative and most applicable projeccts created. They are usually chosen by a panel of judges.

What should I bring?

nwHacks is a 48hour event, which means you will be sleeping overnight. Be sure to bring everything you need to stay the night, including a sleeping bag! All food will be provided at event so don't worry about that!
